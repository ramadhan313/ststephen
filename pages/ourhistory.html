From 1989-Now

In about a decade of existence, those at Unit 731 produced a massive amount of germs, enough to kill the world three times over. Germ weapons such as anthrax, glanders, and the bubonic plague were, in fact, deployed in China during the war. In the Chongshan Village of Yiwu, Zhejiang Province, an epidemic was caused by the IJA's biological weapons, and many innocent civilians perished. Survivors of the incident ended up with rotten legs and, to this day, are unable to live normal lives due to the attack.
